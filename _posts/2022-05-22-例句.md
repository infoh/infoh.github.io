## 例句

辨别修饰关系，感受连贯的语意。

简单说就是锻炼语感。一方面修饰关系存在于词与词之间，需要判断它们的相对地位和亲疏距离。另一方面修饰关系还存在于从句之间，修饰整句还是修饰单个词。



*"Situate your feet about shoulder width apart and facing slightly outward, which is the easiest, most stable stance. "*
from: "The Power of the Squat"

Situate your feet …, **which** is …/// which 从句修饰整个前句。
… apart and facing … /// 通过 and 并列，修饰 feet，分词用主动意味着双脚是“主动”面向，和副词 apart 一起，表示主句宾语 feet 的最终状态或者结果。
about shoulder width 修饰 apart。
slightly outward 修饰 facing。



*"Keeping your feet considerably wider or closer together or angled too far in or out, places potentially worrisome loads on the knees or hips."*
from: "The Power of the Squat"

keeping … places … on …/// 现在分词短语做主语，或叫动名词，places 是谓语。
… wider or closer … or angled … /// 三项并列，修饰 feet，比较级表示相对于肩宽，分词用被动意味着双脚“被”调整角度。
together 修饰 closer，意味着靠近是相互的，但习惯上不说 wider together，也许会说 wider apart。
… in or out 修饰 angled，状语。
… far 修饰 in or out。
too 修饰 far，表示过分、过于之意。



*"Knees should move approximately in line with your toes as you squat -- not splayed outward or buckling in -- and not extending too far past the ends of your toes."*
from: "The Power of the Squat"

… not splayed…or buckling…**and** not extending…/// 修饰 knees，分词形式表状态，注意区分主动和被动，参考点是主语 knees。一定要与主语看齐，根据及物动词与不及物动词确定主动或被动，此外分词短语有可能前置到主语之前。最后注意 and 表并列，与 or 表否定并列有区别，and 是更大范围的并列。
outward, in, too far 各自修饰一个动词。



实际说话时，可以通过语速节率和语气的轻重辅助表达修饰关系。阅读时是否也可以想象一下呢？而且，说话时，感情和用意更多地体现在修饰词上，比如前面的 outward、in、too、together、slightly、considerably 等等看似不起眼的词，说话发音时常常被强调。



*"It is hard to believe that my nephew is 19 when it seems only a short while ago that I was still carrying him in his swaddling clothes, and his sole means of communication was making baby noises and blowing spit bubbles."*
from: "What do The Chinese Eat on Birthday"

(主句) It is hard to believe that ... **when** (从句) ... /// 从句事件发生的时候发生了主句事件

**it seems** ... **that** (从句1) ... and (从句2) ... /// 强调句型 it seems ... that ...；it is ... that ...

a short while ago, I was still carrying, and his ... was ... and ... /// 非强调句型
